About Us

SIXER is a tech non profit organization based in Seattle. We help underrepesnted groups who wouldn't otherwise have access to technology.

One example is helping minorities build virtual reality experiences to tell their story, building empathy and understanding.

We work primarily with underrepesnted groups who wouldn't otherwise have access to this kind of technology.

We want to build a platform that provides tech entrepreneurs with limited resources, access to business training, project capital raising, and mentoring.
