- ABOUT US -

SIXR is a tech non-profit organization based in Seattle. We help underrepresented groups who wouldn't otherwise have access to technology.

We achieve this by helping minorities build virtual reality experiences to tell their story, building empathy and understanding.

We work primarily with underrepresented groups who wouldn't otherwise have access to this kind of technology.

We seek to obtain funding to build a platform that provides tech entrepreneurs with limited resources, access to business training, project capital raising, and mentoring.
